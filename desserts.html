Carrot Halwa

Ingridents:

  • carrot-2 cups(grated)
  • sugar-1 cup
  • ghee(Clarified butter)-4 TS
  • almonds- 6(chopped)
  • cashew nuts-10(chopped)
  • pistachios-6(chopped)

Procedure:

Heat a pan on low flame.Add 2 TS of ghee.Now add almonds,cashew nuts,pistachios and roast them until they turn golden brown.Take out the nuts and keep it aside

Now,in the same pan add the grated carrot and fry them for 5 minutes.Now add sugar and 1 TS of ghee and give a good mix. When carrot starts to leave its water,cover the pan with the lid and cook for 10 minutes.

Now add 1 TS of ghree and stir it.Cook for 3-6 minutes.Add the roasted nuts and give a fine stir.Now the halwa is ready to eat.

Serve the halwa in a bowl

Note:To know whether the halwa is cooked or not ,taste a pinch of halwa,If u can feel the raw carrot then cook it for few more minutes. TS stands for Table Spoon.

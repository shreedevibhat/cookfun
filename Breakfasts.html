Veg French Toast

Ingridents:

  • bread -4 slices
  • oil -2 TS
  • rava(suji)-1/2 cup
  • curd -1/2 cup
  • green chilli-1
  • ginger-1/4 TS(chopped)
  • termaric poweder-1 pinch
  • coriender leaves-1/2 T.S(chopped)
  • onion-1/2 cup(chopped)
  • tomato sauce-1 TS
  • salt- to taste

Procedure:

In a bowl add rava,curd,green chilli,ginger,termaric powder,onion,coriender leaves and salt.Mix them well.Keep it aside for 5 minumtes.

Now take the bread slice and on the top of it spread the batter using a knife or a spoon.Repeat the for rest of the slices.

Now heat a pan and place the bread slice such that batter is facing is on the top.After 2 minutes flip the slice and cook until both the sides of the slice is golden brown

Serve the hot and tasty Veg French Toast with tomato sauce.

Note:Don't make the batter too thin.Ginger is optional.

Tips for easy cooking

Storing coriender

place the washed and dried coriender leaves in a air tight box in fridge

coriender leaves can stay fresh up to 15 days in the fridge

Onion Chopping

After peeling onions cut them into half and soak in water for about 10 minutes before you cut to avoid crying.

You can also wear goggles while chopping

Reduce saltness

Add slices of potato to the gravy dishes to reduce the salt content

another method is by adding small dough of weat flour
